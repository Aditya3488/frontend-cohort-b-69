Step-by-Step Process to Make Maggie Noodles:

  1. Ingredients:
    • 1 packet of Maggie noodles
    • 1 ½ cups of water
    • Maggie tastemaker (comes with the noodle packet)
    • Optional: vegetables (carrot, peas, onions) or eggs for extra flavor
  2. Preparation:
    • Open the packet of Maggie noodles and take out the tastemaker and noodle cake.
  3. Boil Water:
    • In a pan, add 1 ½ cups of water (you can adjust based on how soupy or dry you want the noodles).
    • Bring the water to a boil on medium-high heat.
  4. Add the Noodles:
    • Break the Maggie noodle cake into halves or quarters (depending on your preference) and add it to the boiling water.
  5. Add the Tastemaker:
    • Open the tastemaker packet and sprinkle the seasoning powder into the boiling water.
    • Stir well to make sure the seasoning is evenly mixed.
  6. Cook the Noodles:
    • Let the noodles cook for about 2-3 minutes, occasionally stirring to prevent them from sticking to the bottom of the pan.
    • Check the noodles for tenderness. If you want more soup, add a little more water. If you prefer dry noodles, let the water evaporate.
  7. Final Touches:
    • Once the noodles are cooked to your desired consistency, remove the pan from the heat.
  8. Serve:
    • Transfer the noodles to a bowl or plate and enjoy your delicious Maggie noodles!

Feel free to customize your Maggie noodles with your favorite vegetables or eggs for added flavor
